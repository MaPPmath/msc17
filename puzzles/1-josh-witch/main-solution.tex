\phWorksheet{Solution - Main Puzzle 1}

The solution, \(2\sqrt3\) feet, is shown by the following diagram.

\begin{center}
\begin{tikzpicture}[x=0.3in,y=0.3in]
  \draw[color=gray,very thin,fill=lightgray] (0,0) circle (3);
  \draw[color=gray,very thin,fill=lightgray] (6,0) circle (3);
  \draw[color=gray,very thin,fill=lightgray] ($(3,{3*sqrt(3)})$) circle (3);
  \draw (0,0) -- (3,0) -- (6,0) -- ($(3,{3*sqrt(3)})$) -- (0,0);
  \draw ($(3,{3/sqrt(3)})$) -- (0,0);
  \draw ($(3,{3/sqrt(3)})$) -- (3,0);
  \draw ($(3,{3/sqrt(3)})$) -- (6,0);
  \draw ($(3,{3/sqrt(3)})$) -- ($(3,{3*sqrt(3)})$);
  \draw ($(3,{3/sqrt(3)})$) -- ($(1.5,{1.5*sqrt(3)})$);
  \draw ($(3,{3/sqrt(3)})$) -- ($(1.5,{1.5*sqrt(3)})+(3,0)$);
  \node at (1.7,-0.4) {\small 3};
  \node at (1.6,1.4) {\small \(2\sqrt{3}\)};
  \node at (3.3,0.7) {\small \(\sqrt{3}\)};
\end{tikzpicture}
\end{center}

Each small triangle is \(30\)-\(60\)-\(90\) as they were dissected from
an equilateral \(60\)-\(60\)-\(60\) triangle. The appropriate side lengths
are obtained by scaling the triangle given in the puzzle by a factor of
\(\sqrt3\).

\begin{center}
\begin{tikzpicture}[x=1in,y=1in]
  \draw (0,0) -- node[below] {\(\sqrt3\)}
        (1,0) -- node[above right] {\(2\sqrt3\)}
        ($(0,{sqrt(3)})$) -- node[left] {\(3\)}
        (0,0);
  \draw (0,0.1) -- (0.1,0.1) -- (0.1,0);
  \node at (0.13, 1.25) {\small\(30^\circ\)};
  \node at (0.8, 0.1) {\small\(60^\circ\)};
\end{tikzpicture}
\end{center}
